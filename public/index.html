    Why Study In ITM IHS Panvel?

    ITM offers an array of programs in allied health science, from Undergraduate to Masters, in its Panvel campus. Spread over an acre of land, the campus is a cluster of modern buildings, with separate establishments for classrooms, administrative sections, labs, and more, connected by landscaped gardens and manicured lawns. Separate residential hostel facility is available within the campus for students coming from distant locations.

    Key Features

    • Laboratories, upgraded with all modern facilities, deliver the best learning experience
    • Hostels for outstation students to offer homely comfort away from home
    • Outdoor and indoor game facilities for holistic mental and physical growth of students

    Bachelor of Optometry

    Wish to pursue a career as an Optometrist? Our Bachelor of Optometry Course is designed to build conceptual, analytical, and practical skills, which are required to help you build a successful career as a practitioner, researcher or an academician.

    4 Years + 12 Months of integrated clinical learning

    Bachelors Of Science in Medical Lab Technology

    Medical Laboratory Technology is a branch of medical science responsible for performing laboratory investigations relating to diagnosis, treatment and prevention of disease. With this course, the basic aim is to provide you with knowledge and training that will enable you to work in various lab settings.

    4 Years + 1 Year of integrated clinical learning
    Full Time

    How To Apply

    Step 1

    Go to the official website (www.itm.edu) and click on the Apply Now button.

    Step 2

    Complete the form and begin the registration process

    Step 3

    Aspirants must fill out the application form with the personal information after finishing the registration process. Necessary documents & certificates will be required.

    Step 4
