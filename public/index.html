Why Study In ITM IHS Panvel?

ITM offers an array of programs in allied health science, from Undergraduate to Masters, in its Panvel campus. Spread over an acre of land, the campus is a cluster of modern buildings, with separate establishments for classrooms, administrative sections, labs, and more, connected by landscaped gardens and manicured lawns. Separate residential hostel facility is available within the campus for students coming from distant locations.

Key Features

  • Laboratories, upgraded with all modern facilities, deliver the best learning experience
  • Hostels for outstation students to offer homely comfort away from home
  • Outdoor and indoor game facilities for holistic mental and physical growth of students
